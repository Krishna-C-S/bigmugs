WHAT WE OFFER?

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffea genus. From the coffee fruit, the seeds are separated to produce a stable, raw product: unroasted green coffee.

We offer four varieties:

Arabica

contains almost 60% more lipids and almost twice the amount of sugar.These play an important part in not only the flavor, but the aroma and body of the coffee.

Robusta

contains high caffeine content, at about 2-2.7%, compared to 1-1.5% for Arabica beans. Robusta beans often create a more bitter and earthy-tasting coffee.

Excelsa

a unique, resilient, and productive species.Excelsa does boast a tart, fruitier flavor and is known for showing attributes of both light and dark roast coffees to create a unique profile.

Liberica

a metallic, woody taste, and is generally a little bitter.The shape of the liberica beans is unique among other commercial species and varieties.
